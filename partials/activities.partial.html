Hiking

Whether walking in the woods or scrambling up a rock face, the Green Mountains in VT are home to a wide variety of hiking options.

Mountain biking

Vermont is well known for some of the best mountain biking and lift-served mountain biking in the Northeast. Mt. Snow and Stratton downhill biking will guarantee smiles on dirt-covered faces.

Skiing

Experience the thrill of skiing or snowboarding on a crunchy fresh snow. Whether you are a beginner looking to learn the basics or an expert seeking challenging slopes, skiing in Vermont offers something for everyone. If you're looking for an exhilarating winter adventure, head to Vermont and hit the slopes for an unforgettable skiing and snowboarding experience.

Snowmobiling

Snowmobiling is a popular winter recreational activity in Vermont. It offers a variety of well-maintained snowmobile trails that wind through forests, meadows, and mountainous regions.
